What is Global Warming?

It is the number one problem of the Earth since it is the increase of Earth's temperature due to the greenhouse gases that is polluting the air such as carbon dioxide that the fossil fuels produce. A change that is believed to be permanently changing the Earth's climate

Causes

  • Deforestation, using the forests as fuel, wherein this deforestation releases large amount of carbon.
  • Excessive usage of chemicals for the croplands.
  • The emission of Carbon from power plants of fossil fuel.
  • The emission of carbon dioxide from gasoline for transportation.
  • Methane emissions from animals.

What I can do

  • Controlling emissions
  • Adapting to Climate Change
  • Geoengineering
