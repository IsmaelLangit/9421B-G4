TowardsGreen.org

TowardsGreen.org is a private non-profit organization. It only started with five people who had a great vision and passion to create an Environmental Protection Advocacy in Baguio City, Philippines.

Vision

We vision that every child in the next generation will feel the fresh air, run through green grass, and witness a world towards green.

Goal

  • Protect

    - Our goal is to protect the environment to preserve the beauty of our mothernature and to have a healthy body

  • Educate

    - The people should be educated so that will have knowledge that could surely help the preserverance of the environment

  • Promote

    - We will promote our advocacy to the young people like us for we believe that we are the hope of the country

Environmental Protection

Rules that aims to conserve the natural resources and preserving the beauty of nature, whereas avoiding the extinction of these resources. Because of our bad attitude towards the environment, it has been abused and therefore having negative effects like greenhouse effect, water pollution, air pollution, landslide, acid rain etc. The government then noticed the negative effects and begun to make caution on activities that causes the environmental deterioration.

Why do I need to join?

We believe that joining TowardsGreen.org is just one of the thousand ways to preserve and save our Environment. This is not only an advocacy but also a great responsibility, think of this, how would you feel if the last tree is starting to fall while you are reading this, or let say, "trees", "nature" or any word that is related to Environment is just taught in school and your children just see it on pictures, not on pointing these things personally by you as a parent, and teaching it when he was growing up. These internalizations are the fuel that boosts our passion to build an organization like this. Everyone should have a part, or cooperate, and if you think TowardsGreen.org is your way, JOIN US NOW.
