TowardsGreen.org

TowardsGreen.org is a private non-profit organization. It only started with five people who had a great vision and passion to create an Environmental Protection Advocacy in Baguio City, Philippines.

Vision

We vision that every child in the next generation will feel the fresh air,  run through green grass, and witness a world towards green.

Goal

  • Protect - Our goal is to protect the environment to preserve the beauty of our mothernature and to have a healthy body
  • Educate - The people should be educated so that will have knowledge that could surely help the preserverance of the environment
  • Promote - We will promote our advocacy to the young people like us for we believe that we are the hope of the country
