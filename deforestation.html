What is Deforestation

It is removing or clearing large areas of forest lands so that we could use it for our advantage knowing that there would be a bad impact to the environment.

Causes

  • Agricultural Expansion
  • Livestock Ranching
  • Loggingr
  • Infrastructure Expansion
  • Overpopulation

What I can do

  • Plant trees in your backyard, community garden and to the entire world
  • Adopt a plant based diet
  • Go paperless or don't waste too much paper
  • Recycle items
