What is Climate Change

A change in averages, variability and extremes of the climate that is due to natural processes such as changes in the radiation of the sun or due to the human influences such as changes in the composition of the amosphere and land that is use.

Causes

  • Greenhouse Effect
  • Gases that play a part in climate change
  • Volcanic Eruptions
  • Ocean Currents
  • Solar Radiations
  • Earth Orbital Changes

What I can do

  • Reduce energy use
  • Better walk than use car for transportation
  • Make every drop of watercount
  • Cool wash and hang to dry
  • Switch to "greenpower"
  • Reduce Reuse Recycle
  • Plant trees
